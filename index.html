The chalet is family run, which has been recently refurbished to a high quality. It is the perfect retreat for couples and families of 4. The park itself is within a short walk of mablethorpe centre and is only 14 miles to Skegness with a local bus service. We are currently taking bookings for the 2014 season, the chalet is popular so get in contact with us to make a booking.
